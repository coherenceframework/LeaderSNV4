Your gap
Your coherent core is always transmitting. What varies is how long its signal takes to reach you. At zero resistance, you respond in the moment — aligned without effort. As resistance increases, the signal takes longer to arrive. Minutes. Days. Months. In some cases, a lifetime. The three rings below represent the territories of your whole operating self — Being, Relating, and Creating. The thickness of each ring reflects how much resistance the diagnostic found in that territory.
